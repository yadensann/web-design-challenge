Summary - Latitude vs. Variables

We are reintroducing weather data from past assignments to analyze how the weather changes the closer you get to the equator. We used data from OpenWeatherMap API to configure our own datasets on over 500 cities.

After assembling our data, Matplotlib was used to create visual plots to explain our data further. Temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity were all aspects that were researched. This webpage was made to better organize the data to effeciently showcase our datasets and graphs.
